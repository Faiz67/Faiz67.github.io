Junkout : A Waste Management System One of the biggest crisis of current times is the rapid growth of non-biodegradable waste accumulated on the earth which results in a number of ecological problems. Most of the waste disposed is recyclable but a very small amount of it is recycled leading to issues. The website helps connect the consumer to the recycler and vice-versa. The consumer can choose the kind of waste to dispose and enter their location and a pickup will be scheduled according to their convenience and they get to make money with little effort put into it. The recyclers can join as vendors thus giving them a bigger consumer rate. The idea behind this is to educate and encourage more people to recycle their waste.
Automatic Temperature Check and Limiting Customer Entrance using IoT During the pandemic social distancing and checking of temperature before entering any shop/store has become a need to ensure safety of customers and workers. All types of locations where people congregate to enforce social distancing and identify individuals with high temperatures on account of COVID-19. In response, technology providers have developed automated identity and temperature monitoring tools that allow locations to check people's temperatures. Store managers have to arrange a person simply for checking temperature and ensuring that a limited number of people are inside the shop at any given moment.
